About This App

This portal connects donors and recipients for blood and organ donations. It facilitates finding matches between donors and patients in need, ensuring that no one has to wait long for a lifesaving transplant or transfusion. Together, we can make a difference!

Blood Donation saves lives by providing the necessary blood for surgeries and treatments.

Organ Donation gives people the chance to continue living through selfless acts of generosity.
